Stays

Finding the perfect accommodation in Lakshadweep is essential for ensuring a comfortable and memorable island experience.
From luxurious beach resorts to cozy guesthouses, the archipelago offers a variety of stays to suit every budget and preference.

Beach Resorts

Indulge in luxury and serenity by staying at one of the beach resorts scattered across the islands. These resorts often feature spacious villas or cottages with stunning views of the ocean, as well as amenities such as swimming pools, spa facilities, and gourmet dining options. Immerse yourself in the lap of luxury while surrounded by the natural beauty of Lakshadweep.

Government Tourist Accommodations

Lakshadweep Administration operates tourist accommodations in various islands, providing comfortable rooms at affordable rates. These facilities are often located in picturesque settings, offering easy access to nearby attractions and activities. Advance booking is recommended, especially during peak tourist seasons.

Beachside Camping

For adventurous travelers seeking a closer connection to nature, beachside camping is an excellent option in Lakshadweep. Set up your tent on the pristine sands of secluded beaches, fall asleep to the soothing sound of waves, and wake up to breathtaking sunrise views. Many tour operators offer guided camping experiences complete with equipment rental and delicious campfire meals.
